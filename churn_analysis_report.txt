INTERPRETATION: All engagement metrics are negatively correlated with churn,
meaning higher engagement = lower churn. Customer service interactions are
the only positive predictor - more contacts = more likely to churn.

================================================================================
CONCLUSION
================================================================================

The analysis reveals that ENGAGEMENT is the primary driver of churn, not price.
Churned customers show consistently lower engagement across all metrics:
  - 20% less viewing time
  - 14% shorter sessions
  - 18% less content variety
  - 20% fewer binge sessions

Combined with more customer service issues (+28%), this suggests that users
are becoming frustrated or dissatisfied, leading to disengagement and eventual
churn.

The solution is NOT price reduction, but rather:
  ✓ Improving content quality and variety (especially Horror/Thriller)
  ✓ Enhancing the user experience to reduce support needs
  ✓ Boosting engagement through better recommendations
  ✓ Providing better value at the Basic tier

By addressing these root causes, the streaming service can significantly
reduce the 38.6% churn rate and improve customer lifetime value.
